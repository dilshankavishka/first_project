Ava MAx

Ava Max, born Amanda Ava Koci on February 16, 1994, is an American singer and songwriter. She gained widespread recognition with her breakout single “Sweet but Psycho,” released in August 2018. The song topped charts in 22 countries and reached the top 10 on the US Billboard Hot 100. Known for her powerful voice and pop anthems, Ava Max often emphasizes themes of empowerment and self-expression in her music. Her debut studio album, “Heaven & Hell,” was released in September 2020 and charted well in the UK and the US. She continues to make waves in the music industry with her unique style and individualistic fashion sense. Recently, Ava Max released her second studio album, “Diamonds & Dancefloors,” in January 2023.
